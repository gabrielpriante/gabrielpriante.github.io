LEED, Justice 40 & Frontline Orgs — Spatial Analysis

repo

Spatial analysis quantifying overlap between LEED-certified buildings, Justice 40 neighborhoods, vacant lots, and frontline organizations in Pittsburgh. Found that only 19% of LEED buildings sit within Justice 40 areas, with Mon Valley communities significantly more isolated from green building infrastructure.
